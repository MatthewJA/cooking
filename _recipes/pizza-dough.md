---
ingredients:
  Flour: 2 c
  Yeast: 1 t
  Sugar: 1 t
  Salt: 1/4 t
  Water: 225 mL
  Olive oil: 1 T
instructions:
  - Mix dry ingredients.
  - Add wet ingredients.
  - Knead for one minute.
  - Rest for 10 minutes.
  - Bake hot for 20 minutes.
prepmins: 5
cookmins: 20
title: Pizza Dough
name: Pizza Dough
